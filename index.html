Munister Agency is a full-cycle IT studio: one team designs, builds, ships and keeps things running - no relay race between agencies and no lost context along the way. Since 2024 we've taken 10+ products from a blank page to real production: media platforms people actually read and listen to, ERP systems running real warehouses, fintech and edtech tools handling real users, and archival projects preserving real history.

Web & product, automation & AI, business systems - from a one-page site to an ERP with inventory and payments. Every project has its own domain and real users.

We work as a small team: design, code, launch and support in one pair of hands, no subcontractors. The stack - from static to Next.js, Flask and PostgreSQL - fits the task.
